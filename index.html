Our Story

Our family has been in the home remodeling business since 1985, starting with Carpet Wagon. Over the years, we saw the same story play out again and again: homeowners frustrated by long timelines, poor communication, and contractors who disappeared halfway through the job.

That's why we started Payless Kitchen & Bath — to do it differently. Fewer delays. Clearer communication. A process built around you, not around us. Four decades later, that's still the standard we hold ourselves to on every kitchen, bathroom, and flooring project we take on.

Our Values

Everything starts with putting the homeowner first — transparent, itemized pricing you can actually understand, a dedicated project coordinator who keeps you informed at every stage, and a firm commitment to the timeline we give you in writing.

We use premium materials and back our work with a workmanship guarantee, because a remodel should still look and feel great years from now, not just on installation day. It's the standard behind every project we complete.

Common Questions

What Homeowners Ask Us Most

How long does a typical kitchen or bathroom remodel take?

Most bathroom remodels take 2–3 weeks, and full kitchen remodels typically run 4–6 weeks depending on scope. We'll give you a specific, written timeline before any work begins, and we stand behind it.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. We pull and manage all required permits as part of your project, so you don't have to navigate city processes on your own.

What if I'm not sure about my budget yet?

That's exactly what the free design consultation is for. We'll walk through realistic options at different price points so you can make an informed decision — no pressure, no obligation.

Will I be able to use my kitchen or bathroom during the remodel?

We plan every project to minimize disruption to your daily routine, and we'll walk you through exactly what to expect before work begins.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — 0% interest financing is available for qualified homeowners, in addition to standard payment options. We'll go over what fits your situation during your consultation.
